FAQs to help you get ready to join

What are the minimum qualifications to join Newegg Marketplace?

Newegg Marketplace is perfect for existing sellers that focus on PCs, tech and tech-related products. Check out our application page for more necessary qualification materials: https://sellerportal.newegg.com/static-site/seller/apply.

What are the benefits of shipping orders with Newegg?

Shipped by Newegg (SBN) is our fulfillment program to help you store, pick, pack, and ship orders; Newegg will also handle returns and exchanges and provide you with our 24/7 customer service team.

What can I do to grow my business?

Newegg offers a dynamic platform on our SellerPortal to help sellers optimize their listings, as well as many marketing tools such as promotions, campaigns, sponsored ads, and search engine marketing.

Learn more on https://www.newegg.com/sellers/index.php/resource-library/.

How much does fulfillment cost with Newegg Marketplace?

To estimate your rate, please visit https://www.newegg.com/sellers/index.php/shipped-by-newegg/.

What are the benefits of advertising with Newegg?

Newegg advertising helps sellers reach more consumers and stay competitive in the online eCommerce space.

What does it cost to sell on Newegg?

To view Newegg selling fees, please visit https://sellerportal.newegg.com/selleracademy/knowledge-base/view-referral-fees/.

How long does the onboarding process take?

In general, the onboarding process will take between 3-7 business days.

*New-Seller Savings conditions. Eligible business must be first-time domestic and international Newegg Marketplace sellers who meet all qualification criteria to join the platform and start selling on or after 04/01/2026. Standard commission rates will apply based on product subcategory. During the first 90 days, eligible sellers will receive a rebate equal to the difference between their standard commission rate and 6%, resulting in an effective 6% commission rate on the seller’s assortment during this period. To qualify, participating sellers must complete all onboarding steps. The 90-day rebate period begins on the eligible seller’s account activation date for selling on Newegg.com. Limited time offer. Exclusions apply.
